GIN & COCKTAIL BAR

Locations

The Lounge, 1244 Evesham Road, Astwood Bank

Louise, Karl and Team welcome you to The Lounge!

We took over The Lounge in February 2020 – a month before the pandemic and were forced to go into lockdown, not the best timing, but we are still here and going strong

Through lockdown with all the imposed restrictions we were the first establishment in Redditch to open for takeaway cocktails keeping everyone happy and hydrated; from locals to those passing through on their way home from work.

The Lounge is a small Gin & Cocktail Bar situated on Evesham Road in Astwood Bank that can seat up to 32 cocktail lovers inside with a small seating area at the front for 8 people.

The Lounge @26, 26 High Street, Alcester

Louise, Karl and Team welcome you to The Lounge @26!

Taking over a Coffee Shop during the second lockdown in November 2020 may not have been the best idea but we did it anyway and haven’t looked back! We are now an award winning establishment after being nominated for, and winning Best Café in Warwickshire, Best Café in the Midlands and then Best Café in England in 2021.

During lockdown and restrictions we were able to keep customers warm and caffeinated with takeaway coffees, cakes and food, and took that time to develop the outside area and decorate.

You’ll find us situated on the High Street in Alcester in a quirky Grade II listed building that can seat up to 30 inside, 8 outside on the high street and up to 40 in what we like to call our secret garden.

We are thrilled to announce we were the winners of Best Cafe in England in 2021 and have been recommended on Restaurant Guru!
