GIN & COCKTAIL BAR

Locations

The Lounge @ 1244, Evesham Road, Astwood Bank

Louise, Karl and Team welcome you to The Lounge!

We took over The Lounge in February 2020 – a month before the pandemic and were forced to go into lockdown, not the best timing, but we are still here and going strong

Through lockdown with all the imposed restrictions we were the first establishment in Redditch to open for takeaway cocktails keeping everyone happy and hydrated; from locals to those passing through on their way home from work.

The Lounge is a small Gin & Cocktail Bar situated on Evesham Road in Astwood Bank that can seat up to 32 cocktail lovers inside with a small seating area at the front for 8 people.
